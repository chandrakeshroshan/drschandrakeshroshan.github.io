About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Physics at Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT), Basar, where I have been contributing to research and education since October 2010.

My research focuses on computational materials science, particularly investigating thermal transport properties using first-principles calculations. I specialize in studying lattice thermal conductivity, phonon dynamics, and structural phase transitions in various material systems including layered materials, rocksalt compounds, and alkaline-earth chalcogenides.

Research

Current Research Focus

My research employs ab initio calculations using density functional theory (DFT) to investigate fundamental properties of materials with potential applications in energy conversion and storage. I utilize advanced computational tools including VASP, Quantum ESPRESSO, and various post-processing packages to study:

Thermal Transport

Investigating ultralow lattice thermal conductivity in layered materials like PbIF and PbClF-type compounds for thermoelectric applications. Studying anisotropic thermal conductivity and phonon scattering mechanisms.

High-Pressure Physics

Exploring pressure-induced structural phase transitions, lattice instabilities, and electronic structure changes in alkaline-earth chalcogenides and halofluorides under extreme conditions.

Materials Design

Developing design strategies for low thermal conductivity materials through atomic mass engineering, studying the effect of lone pair activity, and investigating anharmonicity in crystal lattices.

2D & Layered Materials

Characterizing electronic, optical, and thermal properties of layered compounds including Bi2O2S, examining their potential for optoelectronic and energy applications.

Computational Methodologies

  • Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations using VASP and Quantum ESPRESSO
  • Phonon calculations and thermal conductivity using ShengBTE and Phono3py
  • Electronic structure analysis and band structure calculations
  • Molecular dynamics simulations for structural analysis
  • High-throughput materials screening
